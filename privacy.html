Privacy Policy

GottaGo: Bladder Dash  ·  One Realm LLC  ·  Effective date: May 29, 2026
Short version: GottaGo does not require an account. We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information. Game progress is stored locally on your device. The only third-party data sharing is through Google AdMob, which serves ads and may use an advertising identifier on your device.

1. Who We Are

GottaGo: Bladder Dash is developed and published by One Realm LLC. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at hello@onerealmllc.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect no personal information directly. GottaGo does not require you to create an account or provide any identifying information to play.

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game progress (completed levels, daily challenge streaks)
  • Best times for campaign and daily challenge levels
  • Settings preferences (control scheme, selected character)
  • Whether the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase has been made

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of GottaGo displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising network operated by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and use the following to serve relevant ads:

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA on iOS / GAID on Android) — a resettable, device-level identifier used for ad personalization
  • General device information (device model, OS version, screen size, language)
  • Coarse location (country or region) derived from IP address
  • Ad interaction data (views, taps)

Google AdMob's data practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can review and reset your advertising identifier in your device's Settings app.

Purchasing "Remove Ads" ($2.99, non-consumable) disables all ads. No AdMob SDK calls are made once ads are removed.

4. Children's Privacy

GottaGo is rated 4+ (iOS) / Everyone (Google Play). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Because the game requires no account and collects no personal data, it is designed to be safe for all ages.

AdMob is configured to serve non-personalized, child-appropriate ads to all users of this app. Personalized advertising is not enabled.

5. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not receive your payment card details or billing address. Purchase receipts are validated locally or through the respective platform's receipt verification system.

6. Analytics

We do not use any analytics or crash-reporting SDKs in GottaGo. We do not collect gameplay telemetry, session data, or usage statistics.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as described in Section 3 (Google AdMob). We have no data to share beyond what AdMob collects directly on our behalf.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

All game data lives on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. We have no server-side copy to delete.

For AdMob data, refer to Google's data management tools to review or delete data associated with your Google account or advertising ID.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under applicable privacy law (including GDPR, CCPA, and others) to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Because we hold no personal data, most such requests can be fully addressed by uninstalling the app.

For AdMob-related data rights, please contact Google directly or use their privacy tools linked above. For any other questions, email us at hello@onerealmllc.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of GottaGo after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
